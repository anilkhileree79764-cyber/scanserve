How it works

Three steps — from zero to taking orders

📋

1. Add your menu

Log in, add your items with prices and prep times. Takes 5 minutes.

Print one QR sticker per table from the dashboard. Stick it on the table.

📱

3. Customers order

Customer scans QR → menu opens → order placed → you see it on your dashboard instantly.

💰

4. Collect payment

Customer pays at counter with cash or your UPI. No payment gateway fees.
